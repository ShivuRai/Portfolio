Design Process

My design approach is rooted in understanding user needs and business goals. I follow a structured yet flexible process to create meaningful experiences:

Research

Understanding user needs, behaviors, and pain points through interviews, surveys, and competitive analysis.

Ideate

Brainstorming solutions, creating user flows, and sketching concepts to address identified problems.

Design

Crafting wireframes, prototypes, and high-fidelity interfaces that balance aesthetics with functionality.

Test

Validating designs through usability testing, gathering feedback, and iterating for improvement.
